Access /Android/data and /Android/obb on Android 14 without root

Posted on 2023-11-24 Edited on 2023-12-03

Access /Android/data and /Android/obb on Android 14 with Shizuku.

Shizuku(https://shizuku.rikka.app) is a tool that allows you to use certain system APIs without having to root your Android device. It works by granting adb shell permissions to a specific app on your device. This app then acts as a proxy between your device and the system APIs, allowing you to perform actions that would normally require root access.

There is the User manual how to active it.

https://shizuku.rikka.app/guide/setup/
